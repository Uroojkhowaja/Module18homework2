ABOUT ME

Born in 1993 in Mirpurkhas (Pakistan), and belongs to the small city of Sindh, (Jhudo). I am a graduate of Center of excellence art and design Mehran university Jamshoro. I have done my Bachelors in Fine Arts in January 2017. My Major was Painting. My goal is to acquire as many technical skills as i can while exploring art as a more social and public endeavor. I had exhibited my work in different galleries of Pakistan and took part in artist practice program Shakir Ali Museum, Lahore (Pakistan). In 2018, I moved Houston, Texas (America) and Joined Visual art alliance (A community of Houston Artists). I Exhibited my work in Archway Gallery and it was Annual Juried Exhibition in Houston (July 5 - August 3) 2019. My interest and passion for creating art always urged her to work. While in covid time i conducted online session of Eid Greeting Cards making (July 21, 2020). In 2020 When i decided to make my own website i was facing a lot of problems but i was fascinated to learn about design thinking then i decided to learn about making apps and websites and dreamed to be a designer. This motivation takes me to learn UX/UI design.

MY ART WORK

ARTIST STATEMENT

The world around me as i sees it with my vision is nothing but reflection. Wisdom comes from reflection, which makes you dive within. Reflections are everywhere. In many ways, they are not only visual connection but it reflects the society. In fact, the power of reflections can be so strong that often we begin to surround ourselves with people we would most like to emulate mimic. Eventually, the person we wear every day can look like fragments of an external world.   My observations forced me to give a physical existence to my ideas and thus i came up with various artworks which is purely a reflection of my thoughts and beliefs.
